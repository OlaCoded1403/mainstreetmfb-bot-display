About Us
Mainstreet MFB formerly known as Afribank Microfinance Bank Ltd is a private limited liability company incorporated in November 2008.
The bank is licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) to provide Micro-Financial Banking services to the general public.
Vision
To be the leading microfinance bank delivering value to customers and stakeholders.
Mission
To deliver microfinance banking services to customers through innovation, professionalism and excellent customer service.
Who We Are
Mainstreet Microfinance Bank provides banking services to individuals, groups, SMEs and businesses requiring micro credit and other financial services.
Core values include customer focus, professionalism, innovation, integrity and value creation.
