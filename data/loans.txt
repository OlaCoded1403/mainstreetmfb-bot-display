Loans
Loans can be obtained from Mainstreet MFB branches where the customer operates a savings/current account and satisfies loan requirements.
The FAQ states the minimum loan amount is N50,000 and maximum amount is N50,000,000, depending on repayment capacity, verifiable business inventory and other criteria.
Loan requirements include having a business, business location within the bank's lending area, and evidence that the business has existed for the required period.
Loan processing can take less than three working days where requirements are met.
Collateral arrangements are flexible depending on loan size, including household goods, business equipment, goods in stock, vehicles and property.
At least two guarantors may be required.
Existing customers may apply online via the secure portal, after which a relationship officer follows up.
