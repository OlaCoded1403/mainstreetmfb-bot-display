Main Treasure Note
Main Treasure Note is presented as an investment/fixed-income product.
The product is Naira-denominated and structured with agreed tenor and returns.
The Terms & Conditions page describes fixed deposits/Main Treasure Note as Naira-denominated, with minimum tenor of 12 months and interest subject to prevailing CBN rates.
The bank states in an interview that Main Treasure Note was launched in 2018 after it looked inward for funding, and that it is similar to commercial paper except that the bank handled marketing and operation internally.
