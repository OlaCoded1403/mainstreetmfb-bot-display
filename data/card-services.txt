Card Services
Mainstreet MFB offers card services including MasterCard and Verve card services.
Cards can be used for ATM withdrawals, POS transactions and airtime purchase.
The FAQ confirms MainstreetMFB ATM cards can be used on other banks' ATMs, for POS purchases, airtime purchase, and PIN change on any bank's ATM using MainstreetMFB ATM Verve Debit/Credit card.
For disputes such as cash dispense without payment, customers are directed to walk into any Mainstreet MFB branch and request a dispute form through the CSO/E-Business Officer.
