Internet Banking
Online banking is described as a real-time solution that enables customers to access their account 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
Mainstreet MFB account holders can access the Online Banking platform.
The platform is free.
Transactions include balance enquiry, inter-bank transfers, account statement generation and bills payment via NIBSS.
Required infrastructure: a personal computer, tablet, hand-held device or other internet-connected device.
Funds can be transferred to all local banks in Nigeria.
Customers may register on the website by clicking online banking and choosing subscribe to register.
